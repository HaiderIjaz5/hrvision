Privacy Policy

How HRVision protects your data and ensures fair recruitment.

Last Updated: May 2026

1. Data Collection & Smart Master Profiles

We collect personal information (name, contact details), identity verification documents (CNIC, Domicile), and professional history (resumes, degrees, experience) to generate your unified Smart Master Profile. This data is strictly used to seamlessly match you with relevant career opportunities on our portal.

2. AI Processing & Unbiased Ranking

Your uploaded documents are processed securely by our proprietary Deep Learning Natural Language Processing (NLP) engine. Our AI focuses entirely on semantic skill matching to eliminate human bias in the recruitment process. We do not sell your data to third-party data brokers, nor do we use your personal information to train public language models.

3. Enterprise-Grade Security

All passwords are cryptographically hashed before being stored in our MongoDB database. Uploaded files are stored in isolated directories and are protected by strict Role-Based Access Control (RBAC). Only authorized HR Administrators directly associated with the specific jobs you apply to can view your documents.

4. Your Digital Rights

As a candidate, you maintain full control over your data. You have the right to update your Smart Master Profile at any time, withdraw active applications, or request the permanent deletion of your account and all associated documents by reaching out via our Contact Page.
