Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 2026

1. Data Collection

We collect personal information (name, email, phone number) and professional information (resumes, degrees, work history) solely for the purpose of matching candidates with open job requirements.

Your uploaded documents are processed securely by our internal Natural Language Processing engine. We do not sell your data to third-party data brokers or use it to train public language models.

As a candidate, you have the right to request the deletion of your account and associated application data at any time by contacting the system administrator.
